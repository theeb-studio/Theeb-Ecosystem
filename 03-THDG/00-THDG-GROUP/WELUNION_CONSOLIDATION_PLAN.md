# WELUNION CONSOLIDATION & INTEGRATION PLAN
## Absorbing Union Clinics + Integrative Health into the Welunion Platform
**Version 1.0 | March 2026 | Confidential**

---

## 1. WHAT GETS ABSORBED

| Entity | Current State | Target State | Timeline |
|--------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| **Union Clinics (THDG-UCL)** | Operating clinics under own brand/licenses | → WellClinics layer (Welunion's clinic network) | Q1-Q2 2026 |
| **Integrative Health (THDG-IHH)** | Operating home care, CDM, urgent care, maternal | → Welunion operational subsidiary | Q2-Q3 2026 |

## 2. GOVERNANCE POST-ABSORPTION

| Dimension | Union Clinics | Integrative Health |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Legal entity** | Merged into Welunion CR | Retained as subsidiary under Welunion |
| **P&L** | Consolidated into Welunion | Separate sub-P&L under Welunion CFO |
| **Staff** | Transfer to Welunion employment | Transfer to Welunion employment |
| **Brand** | Rebranded to Welunion | Services branded under Welunion divisions |
| **Licenses** | MOH licenses transferred/renewed under Welunion | Home care license retained under subsidiary |
| **Operations** | Managed by WellClinics division head | Managed by Home Care / CDM division heads |

## 3. INTEGRATION WORKSTREAMS

| Workstream | Owner | Q1 2026 | Q2 2026 | Q3 2026 |
|-----------|-------|---------|---------|---------|
| Legal & corporate | Legal counsel | Absorption agreement drafted | Signed and CR updated | Complete |
| HR & staff transition | HR Director | Retention plan; offer letters | 50% transferred | 100% transferred |
| Patient data migration | WellTech CTO | Data audit; PDPL consent review | Migration to WellTech EHR | Complete |
| Brand transition | Marketing | Dual branding permitted | Welunion primary brand | Full rebrand |
| Insurance credentialing | Operations | Credential transfer initiated | Active with major payers | Complete |
| Clinical protocols | CMO | Protocol alignment review | Unified care pathways | CBAHI-ready |
| Financial consolidation | CFO | Separate reporting maintained | Transitional reporting | Consolidated |

## 4. RISK MITIGATION

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Staff attrition during transition | Retention bonuses for key clinical staff; career path visibility |
| Patient disruption | No change to care delivery during transition; gradual brand migration |
| License continuity | MOH engagement early; parallel licensing if needed |
| Data loss | Full backup before migration; phased rollover with validation |
| Revenue gap | Maintain existing payer contracts until Welunion credentialing complete |

## 5. POST-ABSORPTION STRUCTURE

```
WELUNION (THDG-WEL)
│
├── WellTech (Technology Layer)
│   EHR, patient apps, telemedicine, FHIR, CII integration
│
├── WellCare (MSO Layer)
│   Licensing, credentialing, HR, compliance, shared services
│
├── WellClinics (Clinic Network Layer)
│   ├── Former Union Clinics locations (rebranded)
│   ├── New clinic openings (Y1: 11 total)
│   └── Expansion pipeline
│

│   Home visits, CDM field teams, maternal care
│
├── CDM Division (from Integrative Health)
│   Diabetes, hypertension, COPD programs
│
└── TAP Pharmacy (integrated but independent entity)
    In-clinic dispensing at all locations
```
