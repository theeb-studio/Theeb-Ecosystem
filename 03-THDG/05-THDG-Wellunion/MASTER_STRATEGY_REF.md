## 1. STRATEGIC MISSION

Welunion is the **point-of-care data sponge** and the **clinical delivery flagship** of the Theeb Ecosystem. In the 2026 logic:

1. **PHC Privatization Absorber:** Designed to absorb the 2,300 government primary health centers being privatized under Vision 2030 — the largest structural opening in Saudi healthcare history.
2. **Data Generation Engine:** Every patient encounter, prescription, referral, and outcome generates structured data that feeds TVG analytics, TKG research, and TTG intelligence — Welunion is where the ecosystem touches the patient.
3. **Three-Layer Vertical Integration:** Technology (WellTech) + MSO (WellCare) + Clinics (WellClinics) = an integrated platform that can scale nationally while maintaining quality through shared infrastructure.

**Chess Player Role:** Welunion is the delivery piece — the square where strategy meets the patient. It is simultaneously the ecosystem's largest revenue entity (SAR 479M Y5), its primary clinical data source, and its proof of concept that integrated care works. Without Welunion, the ecosystem is theory. With Welunion, it is demonstrated infrastructure.
