# THDG-WEL — WELUNION
**Entity Code: THDG-WEL | Group: Theeb Health Delivery Group**
**Role: FLAGSHIP — Primary platform of THDG**

---

## Identity
Welunion is the **primary platform and flagship company** of the Theeb Health Delivery Group — a large integrated healthcare delivery platform designed as a three-layer system: Technology, MSO (Management Services Organization), and Clinic Network.

> Welunion is the future home of all THDG delivery operations.
> Integrative Health and Union Clinics will be absorbed into or operate under Welunion.

---

## The Three-Layer Architecture

### Layer 1: Technology Platform (WellTech)
- EHR and clinical information systems
- Patient-facing apps (booking, telemedicine, results)
- AI-powered clinical decision support tools
- FHIR R4 integration with NPHIES and TVG data layer
- Data infrastructure feeding TVG Integra RCM and VBH Company

### Layer 2: MSO — Management Services Organization (WellCare)
- MOH facility licenses (held at MSO level)
- Insurance credentialing and CCHI compliance
- HR, payroll, and staff management shared services
- Procurement, supply chain, and logistics
- Quality management and CBAHI preparation
- Compliance framework and regulatory reporting

### Layer 3: Clinic Network (WellClinics)
- Physical clinic locations (hub-and-spoke model)
- Virtual care delivery
- 7 revenue divisions across all locations
- National expansion roadmap
