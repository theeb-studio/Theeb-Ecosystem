# THDG-TAP — TAP PHARMACY LLC
**Entity Code: THDG-TAP | Group: Theeb Health Delivery Group**
**Role: In-clinic dispensing network — standalone entity integrated with Welunion ecosystem**

---

## Identity
TAP Pharmacy is the **in-clinic dispensing network** of THDG — capturing prescription revenue at the point of care and keeping it inside the ecosystem. TAP operates as a distinct legal entity that integrates with the Welunion clinic network and the TVG analytics layers.

## Strategic Logic
Every prescription written at a Welunion / Union Clinics location is fulfilled by TAP.
Revenue that would otherwise exit to external pharmacies stays inside THDG.
This is a **structural margin advantage** built into the ecosystem architecture.
