## 1. STRATEGIC MISSION

TAP Pharmacy is the **prescription revenue capture layer** of the Theeb Ecosystem — capturing dispensing revenue at the point of care while generating structured medication data for the Shared Intelligence Layer. In the 2026 logic:

1. **Revenue Capture at Point of Care:** In-clinic dispensing prevents prescription leakage to external pharmacies — every Welunion patient encounter becomes a potential dispensing event.
2. **Prescription Data Pipeline:** Every prescription generates structured data (medication, dosage, frequency, patient profile) that feeds CII population analytics, VBH Co cost-of-care models, and TVG financial intelligence.
3. **National Scale Path:** Scales automatically with every new Welunion clinic location — embedded infrastructure, not standalone business.

**Chess Player Role:** TAP Pharmacy is the revenue capture piece — it monetizes the clinical encounter at the pharmacy layer while generating one of the most valuable data streams in healthcare (prescription analytics). Small in isolation; strategically critical within the ecosystem.
