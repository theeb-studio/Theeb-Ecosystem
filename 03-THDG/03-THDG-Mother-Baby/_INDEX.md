## Identity
Mother & Baby LLC is the **maternal and postnatal care entity** of THDG — operating the Doula Program and B2C maternal care services. It currently operates as a separate legal entity and is part of the Integrative Health service portfolio that will be absorbed into Welunion.

## Position in the Architecture
```
Welunion (Flagship)

```

## Service Lines

### Doula Program
- B2C cash-pay prenatal and postpartum support bundles
- 100% out-of-pocket — no insurance billing
- High-trust, relationship-driven segment
- Leverages Union Clinics locations + Home Care fleet for in-home postpartum visits

### Maternal Care Services
- Antenatal monitoring and education
- Postnatal support and lactation guidance
- Mental health screening (PPD) — connects to Mental Health Clinical Ventures

## Strategic Value
- Maternal care is a high-trust, premium cash-pay category
- Mother & Baby brand + clinical relationships provide competitive advantage
- Feeds THDG-CLV Mental Health (postpartum depression → mental health clinic referral)
- Data feeds TVG VBH Company (maternal outcomes, GDM, PPD prevalence)
