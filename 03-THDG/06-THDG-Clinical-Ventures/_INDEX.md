# THDG-CLV — INDEPENDENT CLINICAL VENTURES
**Entity Code: THDG-CLV | Group: Theeb Health Delivery Group**
**Role: Specialized clinical entities — independent, NOT part of Integrative Health or Welunion by default**

---

## Identity
Independent Clinical Ventures are **specialized clinical entities** that operate with flexibility in their relationship to the broader THDG ecosystem. They are NOT automatically subsidiaries of Welunion or Integrative Health.

> **Key principle:** Do not assume these ventures are owned or operated by Welunion.
> Each venture has its own ownership structure defined individually.

---

## Flexible Ownership Framework

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Fully owned** | 100% Theeb-owned and operated |
| **Partially owned** | Joint venture with physician, clinical partner, or investor |
| **Partner-operated** | Partner owns and operates; Welunion provides MSO/infrastructure services |
| **Externally owned, managed** | External owner; Welunion or THDG provides management services only |

## Integration Architecture

The three THDG front-line entities (Welunion, CoClinics, Dawali) operate as an integrated referral drawer:
- **Level 1 — Welunion:** Primary care, CDM, digital health — detects and refers up
- **Level 2 — CoClinics:** Shared specialty clinics — intermediate complexity, refers up or returns down
- **Level 3 — Dawali:** Interventional procedures — highest complexity, returns patients to Welunion for follow-up

## Why Independent vs. Inside Welunion?

Keeping ventures independent allows:
- Physician partnership structures (doctors co-own their specialty clinic)
- External investor participation in specific ventures
- Faster regulatory approvals (separate CR for specialty practice)
- Protection of Welunion's MSO model from specialty regulatory complexity

They still benefit from the ecosystem:
- Welunion MSO services (HR, compliance, credentialing)
- Welunion technology layer (EHR, booking, telemedicine)
- TAP Pharmacy (in-house dispensing)
- TKG Nama Academy (CME and staff training)
- TVG data infrastructure (outcomes measurement)
