## 1. STRATEGIC MISSION

Integrative Health is the **operational backbone** currently powering THDG's active clinical services — home care, chronic disease management, urgent care, and maternal services. In the 2026 logic:

1. **Operational Continuity:** Maintains active patient care and revenue while Welunion's three-layer architecture is being built.
2. **Welunion Integration:** Transitions its operations, staff, and patient base into Welunion's WellClinics layer — providing the operational starting point for Welunion's national expansion.
3. **Data Generation:** Currently the primary source of home care and CDM outcomes data for VBH Co and CII.

**Chess Player Role:** Integrative Health is a transitional piece — it holds the delivery square while Welunion is assembled. Once absorbed, its operational capabilities become permanent infrastructure within the Welunion platform.
