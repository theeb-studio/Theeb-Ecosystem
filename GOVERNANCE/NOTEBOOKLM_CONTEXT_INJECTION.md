## THE ECOSYSTEM IN 60 SECONDS

**What:** Theeb is a network of 11 specialized healthcare companies organized into 4 strategic groups, designed to be orchestrated together as integrated solutions.

**Why:** Saudi Arabia spends SAR 196B on healthcare while importing all clinical knowledge. Theeb fills this gap by owning the full value chain: knowledge production (TKG) → financial intelligence (TVG) → clinical delivery (THDG) → AI infrastructure (TTG).

**How:** The Chess Player (Holding) controls the Shared Intelligence Layer — data standards (FHIR R4), regulatory relationships (SFDA, SCFHS, CHI, SAMA), and capital allocation. Each subsidiary operates autonomously but generates maximum value through ecosystem orchestration.

**When:** Three market windows are open simultaneously in 2026:
1. AR-DRG implementation (hospital payment reform) → Integra RCM
2. PHC privatization (2,300 centers) → Welunion
3. Insurer loss ratios at 83-96% → VBH Co + Nexus MGA

## THE ORCHESTRATION PRINCIPLE

The power is NOT in any single entity. It is in the orchestration.

- A pharma company can hire any CRO. They cannot hire an ecosystem that simultaneously runs the trial, publishes the results, trains the physicians, delivers the drug in clinics, measures the outcomes, and negotiates the insurance coverage.
- A hospital can hire any billing company. They cannot hire one that also measures their outcomes, designs their insurance contracts, trains their staff, and provides clinical AI at the point of care.
- The Lead Entity Rule: whichever company is closest to the client's primary need leads the engagement. Others activate on need. Revenue is pre-agreed.
