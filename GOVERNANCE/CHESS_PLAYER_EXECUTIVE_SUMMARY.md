## THE OPENING (2026 Moves)

### Move 1: AR-DRG Readiness
**Piece:** Integra RCM (TVG-01)
**Square:** CHI mandates AR-DRG implementation for all hospitals
**Play:** Position as the readiness partner. Every hospital RCM client becomes a pipeline for VBH Co analytics and Nexus MGA insurance products. Integra RCM is the door-opener for the full TVG stack.

### Move 2: PHC Privatization
**Piece:** Welunion (THDG-WEL)
**Square:** 2,300 government primary health centers privatizing
**Play:** Welunion's three-layer architecture (technology + MSO + clinics) is designed to absorb privatized facilities. Asset-light model: SAR 500K per clinic, breakeven in 7 months. Every clinic generates data for CII and claims for Integra RCM.

### Move 3: Insurer Crisis Resolution
**Pieces:** VBH Co (TVG-03) + Nexus MGA (TVG-02)
**Square:** Insurer loss ratios at 83-96% — unsustainable
**Play:** VBH Co measures what healthcare actually delivers (PROMs, cost-of-care). Nexus MGA designs insurance products priced on real outcomes, not actuarial guesswork. Together they build Saudi Arabia's first value-based payment infrastructure.

### Move 4: Scientific Society Network
**Pieces:** Waraqa (TKG-01) + Nama Academy (TKG-03) + Evidence Platform (TKG-05)
**Square:** 80+ Saudi scientific societies with no operational infrastructure
**Play:** Offer turnkey partnership — Waraqa runs the journal, Academy delivers CME, Platform hosts the digital hub. Society keeps 40-60% of revenue. Zero operational burden. Category A SCFHS rating guaranteed. Each society partnership locks in physician access for the Evidence Platform.

### Move 5: Physician Network Lock-In
**Pieces:** Nama Academy (TKG-03) → Evidence Platform (TKG-05)
**Square:** SCFHS mandates 30 CME credits/year for every health professional
**Play:** Every physician trained through Nama Academy gets an Evidence Platform account ($0 CAC). At 2,000+ physicians/year, the platform reaches 10,000+ users in 5 years — creating an irreplaceable distribution moat.
