# NEXUS MGA — MASTER REFERENCE
**Entity Code: TVG-02 | Group: Theeb Value Group (TVG)**
**Version 1.0 | March 2026 | Confidential**

---

## CORE MISSION

Design and distribute data-driven insurance products as a Managing General Agent — building Saudi Arabia's first risk infrastructure layer that uses clinical intelligence, outcomes data, and population analytics to create actuarially sound, value-aligned health insurance products.

---

## STRATEGIC MOAT

| Moat Layer | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Data Advantage** | Access to TVG claims data (Integra RCM) + CII population analytics — no traditional MGA has this |
| **Malpractice + CME Bundle** | Unique product combining malpractice insurance with CME education hours (SAR 800-1,200/physician/year) — no competitor offers this |
| **SAMA Licensing Pathway** | Early-stage positioning for MGA license under SAMA regulatory framework |
| **Ecosystem Integration** | Insurance products informed by real clinical outcomes, not just actuarial tables |
| **Employer Health Benefits** | Data-driven employer health benefit design using population health intelligence |
