# EVIDENCE PLATFORM — MASTER REFERENCE
**Entity Code: TKG-05 | Group: Theeb Knowledge Group (TKG)**
**Version 1.0 | March 2026 | Confidential**

---

## CORE MISSION

Operate Saudi Arabia's sovereign clinical evidence platform — the digital layer that distributes clinical guidelines, CME content, real-world data analytics, and clinical decision support to physicians and institutions at scale. Previously incubated inside Waraqa; transitioning to independent entity.

---

## STRATEGIC MOAT

| Moat Layer | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Knowledge Graph** | 16,290+ medical entity nodes with 31,913+ relationship edges — growing exponentially |

| **Zero-CAC Distribution** | Every Nama Academy trainee gets a platform account — 2,000+ physicians/year at $0 acquisition cost |
| **MNC Decision Engine** | 70 interactive Medical Necessity Criteria from CHI — transforms static PDFs into clinical workflow tools |
| **Content Pipeline** | Fed by Waraqa publications, Nama CRO trial data, and Nama Bio PK data — no content acquisition cost |
