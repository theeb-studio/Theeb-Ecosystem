**Entity Code: THDG-WEL | Group: Theeb Health Care Delivery Group (THDG)**
**Version 1.0 | March 2026 | Confidential**
**Status: FLAGSHIP entity of THDG**

---

## CORE MISSION

Operate Saudi Arabia's integrated primary care and chronic disease platform — combining physical clinics, 24/7 virtual care, home visits, pharmacy, mental health, maternal care, and chronic disease management under one connected brand. Welunion absorbs Union Clinics and Integrative Health into a three-layer platform architecture.

---

## STRATEGIC MOAT

| Moat Layer | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **PHC Privatization Timing** | Vision 2030 privatizing 2,300 primary health centers — structural opening |
| **Three-Layer Architecture** | Technology (WellTech) + MSO (WellCare) + Clinics (WellClinics) = vertically integrated platform |
| **Ecosystem Data Generation** | Every patient encounter generates data for TVG analytics, TKG research, and TTG intelligence |
| **Asset-Light Scale** | SAR 500K buildout per clinic; 7-month breakeven per clinic |
| **7 Revenue Divisions** | Diversified across urgent care, home care, virtual care, CDM, mental health, men's health, maternal care |

## THREE-LAYER ARCHITECTURE

```
WELUNION
|
+-- Layer 1: WellTech (Technology Platform)
|   EHR, patient apps, telemedicine, AI clinical tools,
|   FHIR integration, data infrastructure
|
+-- Layer 2: WellCare (MSO — Management Services Organization)
|   MOH licenses, insurance credentialing, HR, compliance,
|   shared services for all clinic entities
|
+-- Layer 3: WellClinics (Clinic Network)
    Physical and virtual clinic assets, 7 revenue divisions,
    national expansion. Absorbs Union Clinics and Integrative Health.
```
