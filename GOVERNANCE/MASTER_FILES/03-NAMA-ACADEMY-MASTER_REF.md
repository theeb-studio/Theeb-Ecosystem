## CORE MISSION

Build Saudi Arabia's healthcare professional workforce through SCFHS-accredited training, CME/CPD delivery, life support certifications, and simulation-based programs — while serving as a zero-cost distribution channel for the Evidence Platform.

---

## STRATEGIC MOAT

| Moat Layer | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **SCFHS Accreditation** | Sole accreditation required for all Saudi healthcare CME — regulatory lock-in |
| **Physician Network Effect** | Every physician trained = future Evidence Platform user at $0 acquisition cost |
| **Society Faculty Model** | Clinician-led credibility — key leaders are practicing clinicians with peer-level access to societies, hospitals, and government |
| **Insurance Bundle** | MGA malpractice + CME bundle (SAR 800-1,200/physician/year) creates unique cross-group product |
| **Mandate Volume** | SCFHS requires every licensed health professional to earn 30 CME credits/year — guaranteed recurring demand |
