# NAMA BIOEQUIVALENCE CENTER — MASTER REFERENCE
**Entity Code: TKG-04 | Group: Theeb Knowledge Group (TKG)**
**Version 1.0 | March 2026 | Confidential**

---

## CORE MISSION

Establish Saudi Arabia's bioequivalence and drug development infrastructure — providing SFDA-regulated BE studies that generate pharmacokinetic data on Saudi populations and accelerate drug localization under Vision 2030's pharmaceutical sovereignty agenda.

---

## STRATEGIC MOAT

| Moat Layer | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **SFDA Regulatory Position** | BE center application filed — one of a handful of accredited facilities in KSA |
| **Asset-Light Model** | 40-bed clinical ward from CARE Medical + bioanalytics from KSU = zero capex |
| **Saudi Population PK Data** | Pharmacokinetic data on Saudi populations is irreplaceable and accumulates with each study |
| **Drug Localization Mandate** | Vision 2030 NIDLP pharmaceutical manufacturing targets create guaranteed demand for BE studies |
| **Zero External Equity** | Funded by 2M SAR government grant — 100% ownership retained |
