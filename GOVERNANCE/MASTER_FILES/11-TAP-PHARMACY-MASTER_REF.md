# TAP PHARMACY — MASTER REFERENCE
**Entity Code: THDG-TAP | Group: Theeb Health Care Delivery Group (THDG)**
**Version 1.0 | March 2026 | Confidential**

---

## CORE MISSION

Capture prescription revenue at the point of care through an in-clinic dispensing network — integrating pharmacy operations into the Welunion ecosystem while providing prescription analytics data to TVG and TTG intelligence layers.

---

## STRATEGIC MOAT

| Moat Layer | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Point-of-Care Dispensing** | Pharmacy inside the clinic captures the prescription at the moment of prescribing — reduces leakage to external pharmacies |
| **Prescription Data** | Every prescription generates structured data feeding population health models |
| **National Scale Path** | Expandable with every new Welunion clinic location |
| **Ecosystem Revenue Capture** | Pharmacy revenue adds margin to clinic economics without additional patient acquisition |
