# WARAQA PUBLISHING HOUSE — MASTER REFERENCE
**Entity Code: TKG-01 | Group: Theeb Knowledge Group (TKG)**
**Version 1.0 | March 2026 | Confidential**

---

## CORE MISSION

Build and operate Saudi Arabia's sovereign scientific publishing infrastructure — launching peer-reviewed journals for scientific societies, developing clinical guidelines, and governing the quality, synthesis, and dissemination of Saudi clinical knowledge.

---

## STRATEGIC MOAT

| Moat Layer | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Publishing Credibility** | 150+ projects, 2,000+ publications, IJMDC journal — track record no Saudi competitor can match |
| **Society Lock-In** | Revenue-sharing model (society keeps 40-60%) creates long-term partnerships with zero operational burden on the society |
| **Content Supply Chain** | Waraqa's publications are the initial knowledge corpus for the Evidence Platform and CII — making Waraqa the content engine of the entire ecosystem |
| **AI Content Licensing** | First-mover on negotiating AI rights on Saudi research content — SAR 100M+ TAM with near-100% margin |
| **Regulatory Relationships** | Clarivate, ProQuest, EBSCO partnerships for indexing; SCFHS alignment for guideline accreditation |
