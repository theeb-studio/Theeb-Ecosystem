## II. MISSION & THESIS

### Mission
Build Saudi Arabia's sovereign clinical knowledge infrastructure — making the Kingdom a producer of clinical evidence, not merely a consumer.

### Core Thesis
Saudi Arabia spends $50-67B annually on healthcare while importing virtually all clinical knowledge from Western evidence bases. TRG fills this gap through an integrated ecosystem where four operating companies generate different types of clinical knowledge that compound over time through a shared Knowledge Platform (FASL).

### Strategic Thesis — Zero-Dilution Ecosystem
TRG is designed so that only ONE company requires external equity investment — the services company with no proprietary IP (NAMA Medical). All other companies are funded through government grants, strategic partnerships, and inter-company revenue. This preserves 100% ownership of the ecosystem's highest-value IP assets (FASL, Waraqa, NAMA Knowledge, NAMA Bio).

## V. FASL KNOWLEDGE PLATFORM

### What FASL Is
The sovereign clinical intelligence platform that connects all operating companies into a compounding knowledge system. FASL is TRG's highest-value long-term asset — the technology layer that transforms a holding company into infrastructure.

### Current State (as of March 2026)
FASL MVP is live at nibrashealth.com (temporary name: Nibras Health — now retired):

| Metric | Current (MVP) | Target — 12 Months |
|--------|--------------|-------------------|
| Indexed Scientific Papers | 2,626 | 50,000+ |
| Medical Knowledge Nodes | 16,290 | 250,000+ |
| Knowledge Edges | 31,913 | 600,000+ |
| Institutions | 10+ | 150+ |
| Source Journals | IJMDC only | 15 Saudi journals + PubMed + Cochrane + WHO |
| Language Support | English only | Arabic + English (full bilingual) |
| MNC Module | Not built | 70 interactive Medical Necessity Criteria |
| CME / Training | Not built | Personalized Learning Passport linked to SCFHS |

### Core Product — Interactive MNC Decision Engine
FASL transforms the Council of Health Insurance's (CHI) 70 Medical Necessity Criteria from static PDF documents into an interactive clinical decision engine. Three interaction tiers:
1. **Reference Reading**: Structured MNC view with evidence-graded recommendations
2. **AI Conversation**: Free-text clinical questions answered with cited evidence
3. **Documented Decision**: Step-by-step patient-specific workflow producing documented outcomes for insurance justification

### Additional Modules
- **CME Learning Passport**: Personalized training plans built from practitioner behavior, specialty profile, and CHI/Nafis national priorities — linked to SCFHS accreditation
- **Research Question Builder**: Evidence gaps detected by FASL become structured PICO research questions routed to Waraqa for publication
- **Institutional Analytics**: Usage data, recurring questions, collective knowledge gap heatmaps per institution
