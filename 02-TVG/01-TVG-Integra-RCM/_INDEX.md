# TVG-RCM — INTEGRA RCM
**Entity Code: TVG-01 | Group: Theeb Value Group | Layer: 1 — Revenue Infrastructure**

---

## Identity
Integra RCM is the **revenue cycle management and clinical coding platform** for healthcare providers — the entry point of the TVG platform and the primary data acquisition engine for the entire four-layer architecture.

## Why It Comes First
- Solves the most immediate provider pain: revenue leakage and coding inaccuracy
- Every coded claim generates structured clinical-financial data for Layers 3 and 4
- Deep HIS integration creates switching costs (5–7 year lock-in per contract)
- Cash-generating from day one — funds development of upper layers

## Core Capabilities
- AI-assisted clinical coding (ICD-10, CPT, SNOMED, AR-DRG)
- Claims management and automated re-submission
- Denial analytics and pattern recognition
- Revenue intelligence dashboard
- AR-DRG readiness (preparation for 2026 CHI implementation)
- CDI (Clinical Documentation Improvement) with NLP

## Revenue Model
- Percentage of net collections: 2–4%
- Fixed monthly platform fee + per-claim processing
- Performance-based bonuses on denial recovery

## Data Output to Upper Layers
→ Claim-level cost data → Layer 3 cost analytics
→ DRG distribution → Layer 4 bundle pricing
→ Payer mix and contract terms → Layer 2 MGA underwriting

## Strategic Layer (this folder)
Strategy, proposals, scope of work, financial reports, client contracts.

## Operational Layer
→ `Dropbox/Server/` (Integra operational files)
- Proposals and contracts

## Key Regulatory Context
- AR-DRG implementation: CHI target 2026 → Integra is the readiness partner
- NPHIES integration: native connector to national claims platform
- NDMO compliance: all data processed and stored in KSA
