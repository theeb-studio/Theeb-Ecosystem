## 1. STRATEGIC MISSION

Integra RCM is the **revenue infrastructure layer** of the Theeb Ecosystem — Layer 1 of TVG's four-layer financial operating system. In the 2026 logic:

1. **AR-DRG Readiness Partner:** Positioned as the primary implementation partner for CHI's 2026 AR-DRG mandate — the single most disruptive payment reform in Saudi healthcare history.
2. **Door-Opener:** Every hospital RCM client becomes a pipeline for Nexus MGA (Layer 2) insurance products and VBH Co (Layer 3+4) outcomes analytics — Integra RCM is the wedge that opens the full TVG stack.
3. **Data Generator:** Claims, coding patterns, and denial analytics flow upward through all TVG layers and into CII's revenue cycle intelligence.

**Chess Player Role:** Integra RCM occupies the revenue recovery square — the first entry point into any hospital relationship. Once embedded in billing workflows, the switching costs make the position permanent, and the data flows enable the rest of TVG.
