## 1. STRATEGIC MISSION

Integra RCM is the **revenue infrastructure layer** that enables every downstream reform in Saudi healthcare. In the 2026 "Perfect Storm" logic, three regulatory forces converge simultaneously — and Integra RCM is the entity positioned at the intersection of all three:

| Regulatory Force | What It Demands | What Integra RCM Provides |
|-----------------|----------------|--------------------------|
| **AR-DRG Mandate (CHI 2026)** | Hospitals must classify inpatient episodes, understand casemix, and report standardized cost data | Shadow billing infrastructure → DRG maturity roadmap → full DRG readiness |
| **CHI VBC Guidelines v3** | Providers must implement bundled payments across 7 clinical areas with outcome measurement and phased risk transfer | VBC contract operational support; PROMs data pipelines; coding accuracy for bundle pricing |
| **Insurer Loss Ratios (83-96%)** | Payers demand cost transparency, reduced variation, and outcome accountability | Claims analytics; cost benchmarking; denial pattern intelligence |

**Chess Player Role:** Integra RCM is the piece that must move first. AR-DRG requires coding infrastructure. VBC requires cost baselines. Outcomes measurement requires clean documentation. Every reform pathway runs through the revenue cycle — and Integra RCM is the only entity in the ecosystem (and one of very few in KSA) that can operationalize all three simultaneously.

## 4. CHI VBC BUNDLED PAYMENT PROGRAMS — OPERATIONAL CAPABILITY

### The 7 CHI-Supported Clinical Areas

Integra RCM provides the **coding, costing, and data infrastructure** required for each of CHI's seven bundled payment programs:

| # | Bundle | Type | Integra RCM Capability |
|---|--------|------|----------------------|
| 1 | **Total Knee Replacement** | Procedural (episode-based) | DRG-based cost baseline; complication rate tracking; readmission window coding; severity-level risk adjustment |
| 2 | **Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy** | Procedural | Episode coding (start: admission → end: 30-day post-discharge); included/excluded service coding; LOS benchmarking |
| 3 | **Tonsillectomy with Adenoidectomy** | Procedural | Pediatric coding accuracy; complication rate analytics; same-day discharge tracking |
| 4 | **Spinal Fusion** | Procedural | High-cost outlier identification; severity-level risk adjustment; device cost separation |
| 5 | **Laparoscopic Sleeve Gastrectomy** | Procedural | BMI-adjusted coding; complication tracking; 90-day readmission window |
| 6 | **Pregnancy and Childbirth** | Procedural | Maternal pathway coding; C-section vs vaginal delivery DRG split; neonatal complication tracking |
| 7 | **Diabetes Management Program** | Condition-based (longitudinal) | Chronic disease episode definition; HbA1c outcome tracking; multi-visit cost aggregation; PROMs data pipeline |

### VBC Phase Alignment

Each bundle progresses through CHI's three VBC phases. Integra RCM supports **every phase transition**:

| VBC Phase | CHI Definition | Integra RCM Role |
|-----------|---------------|------------------|
| **Phase 1: Report & Measure** | Fixed bundled payments; no financial risk; establish data collection and baseline performance | Shadow billing; baseline cost calculation; coding standardization; PROMs collection infrastructure |
| **Phase 2: Share Risk** | Shared savings; providers earn incentive payments above MSR; no downside risk | MSR calculation using DRG cost baselines; performance dashboards; coding accuracy for savings validation |
| **Phase 3: Own Outcomes** | Two-sided risk; upside gains and downside losses linked to performance | Full risk analytics; stop-loss calculation; risk corridor management; outcome-to-cost reconciliation |

### CHI Gating Criteria — How Integra RCM Ensures Phase Progression

| Gating Criterion | CHI Threshold | Integra RCM Solution |
|-----------------|--------------|---------------------|
| Data completeness (outcomes) | 85% | CII-powered documentation ensures near-complete capture |
| PROMs collection rate | 70% | VBH Co PROMs infrastructure integrated with Integra claims data |
| On-time settlement | 100% | AI-powered claims submission with pre-claim scoring |
| Coding accuracy | Not specified (implied >90%) | CII Layer 4-5: >95% target via automated documentation + pre-claim scoring |
| Governance capability | Mature | CDI program + outcomes dashboards |
| Financial management | Mature | DRG-based cost reporting + MSR calculation |

### Revenue Model — The Gain-Share Engine

Integra RCM operates a **hybrid revenue model** combining predictable SaaS-style fees with performance-based gain-share revenue that scales directly with client value delivered:

| Revenue Stream | Type | Timing | Revenue Logic |
|---------------|------|--------|---------------|
| RCM operations (per-claim fee) | Recurring SaaS | Immediate | Predictable base revenue from Day 1 |
| DRG readiness advisory | Project-based | 2026-2028 | One-time engagement fees |
| CDI program implementation | Subscription + project | 2026+ | Monthly retainer + setup |
| VBC bundle operations | Subscription | 2027+ | Per-bundle operational fee |
| Pre-claim scoring (CII) | Per-claim fee | 2027+ | Volume-based, scales with encounters |
| **Gain-Share: DRG Optimization** | **% of verified savings** | **Phase 2+** | **15-25% of revenue recovered through improved coding accuracy, reduced denials, and DRG optimization** |
| **Gain-Share: VBC Shared Savings** | **% of verified savings** | **VBC Phase 2-3** | **15-25% of savings generated above MSR in CHI bundled payment programs** |

### The Gain-Share Principle

> **Revenue scales with client value, not headcount.**
>
> Traditional RCM companies charge per-claim fees regardless of impact. Integra RCM aligns incentives: the more savings we generate, the more we earn. This creates a **Closed Loop Revenue Engine** where performance compounds quarterly.

**How it works:**

