### CHALLENGE 1: Structural Overlaps & Duplication of Functions

**CHI's Problem:**
Policy design, actuarial analysis, monitoring, and stakeholder engagement are duplicated across three departments (Beneficiaries & Employers, Sector Empowerment, Service Providers). **Why This Matters for DRG/VBC:**
- DRG costing needs consistent actuarial models — not three competing ones
- VBC bundle pricing requires unified cost baselines — not department-specific estimates
- Providers receive contradictory requirements from different departments

**How Theeb Resolves This:**

### CHALLENGE 5: VBC-DRG Implementation Confusion

**CHI's Problem (from DRG-VBC Alignment Assessment):**
Implementing DRGs and VBC bundles simultaneously creates five operational risks:
1. Payment confusion and mixed incentives
2. Coding and documentation burden
3. Financial volatility and provider sustainability
4. Fragmentation of care pathways
5. Market readiness and change fatigue

**How Theeb Resolves Each Risk:**

| Risk | CHI Mitigation Strategy | Theeb Operational Resolution |
|------|------------------------|------------------------------|
| **Payment confusion** | CHI circular clarifying no double payment | Integra RCM enforces: bundles = primary for 7 VBC programs; DRG = standard for all other inpatient. System-level enforcement, not circular-based. |
| **Coding burden** | Align data requirements | CII Layer 4 automates documentation. One coding infrastructure serves both DRG assignment and VBC episode coding. ICD-10-AM + SBS used for both. |
| **Financial volatility** | Phased financial exposure | Integra RCM's Shadow Billing → Transparency → DRG-Ready roadmap mirrors CHI's own phased approach. VBC Phase 1 (no risk) → Phase 2 (upside only) → Phase 3 (two-sided). Financial safeguards built into contracts. |
| **Care pathway fragmentation** | Define clear episode boundaries | VBH Co defines standardized episode boundaries for all 7 bundles (start/end points, inclusions/exclusions, readmission windows). Evidence Platform provides clinical guidelines for each pathway. |
| **Change fatigue** | Stagger timelines | Theeb deploys ONE integrated solution (not 5 separate change programs). The TVG stack enters as revenue recovery (immediate ROI) and layers on DRG/VBC capabilities progressively. |
