# TVG-MGA — MGA COMPANY (NEXUS MGA)
**Entity Code: TVG-02 | Group: Theeb Value Group | Layer: 2 — Risk Infrastructure**

---

## Identity
The MGA Company is TVG's **Managing General Agent** — a data-driven risk platform that designs and manages healthcare insurance products using clinical intelligence that traditional insurers cannot replicate. It sits between insurance carriers and healthcare providers, creating better-priced, outcome-linked products.

## What Makes This MGA Different
Traditional insurers price on historical utilization. TVG MGA prices on:
1. Real provider cost data (from Integra RCM, Layer 1)
2. Outcome probability models (from VBH Company, Layer 3)
3. Population risk scores built on live clinical data

This creates a **structural underwriting advantage** — fairer prices for providers, better margins for carrier partners.

## Core Capabilities
- Underwriting analytics engine (clinical data-driven)
- Alternative risk products: capitation, bundled-episode insurance, employer self-insurance
- Provider-payer integration layer (API between RCM and payer claims)
- Population health risk scoring (chronic disease, maternal, mental health)
- Longevity and preventive benefit design

## Key Product: Malpractice + CME Bundle
In partnership with TKG Nama Academy:
- MGA designs malpractice/professional liability insurance product
- Product bundled with 30–60 SCFHS-accredited CME hours (fulfilled by Nama Academy)
- Physician buys one product → gets legal coverage + SCFHS compliance
- Revenue: MGA earns underwriting commission; Nama earns SAR 800–1,200/physician/year

- Administration fees on self-insured employer schemes
- Risk-sharing arrangements: profit commission on favorable loss ratios
