## 1. BUNDLE PRODUCT DESIGN

| Component | Provider | Detail |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| **Malpractice Insurance** | Underwritten by licensed insurer (via Nexus MGA) | Coverage: professional liability per SAMA standards |
| **CME Credits** | Fulfilled by Nama Academy (TKG-03) | 30 SCFHS credits/year included in bundle |
| **Evidence Platform Access** | Included (TKG-05) | Learning Passport + clinical decision support |
| **Price** | SAR 800-1,200/physician/year | Competitive vs standalone malpractice (SAR 1,500-3,000) |

## 2. PHYSICIAN SIGN-UP WORKFLOW

```
STEP 1: Physician receives bundle offer
        (via society partnership, Nama Academy training, or Welunion employment)

STEP 2: Signs up on Nexus MGA portal
        → Identity verified via Evidence Platform SSO
        → Specialty, license number, SCFHS status confirmed

STEP 3: Malpractice policy issued
        → Underwriter activates coverage
        → Policy documents to physician

STEP 4: CME access activated
        → Nama Academy assigns 30-credit learning pathway
        → Learning Passport tracks progress on Evidence Platform
        → Physician accesses CME courses (online + live)

STEP 5: Quarterly verification
        → Nama Academy reports CME completion status to Nexus MGA
        → If <25% progress at Q2 → reminder notification
        → If <50% progress at Q3 → escalation notification
        → Year-end: CME fulfillment certified or remediation

STEP 6: Annual renewal
        → Auto-renewal if CME fulfilled + no active claims
        → Premium adjustment based on claims history
```

## 3. REVENUE FLOWS

```
Physician pays SAR 1,000/year to Nexus MGA
    │
    ├── SAR 400 → Underwriter (malpractice premium)
    ├── SAR 300 → Nama Academy (CME fulfillment fee)
    ├── SAR 100 → Evidence Platform (platform access fee)
    └── SAR 200 → Nexus MGA (margin)

AT SCALE (10,000 physicians):
    Total revenue: SAR 10M/year
    Nama Academy share: SAR 3M/year
    Evidence Platform share: SAR 1M/year
    Nexus MGA margin: SAR 2M/year
    Underwriter premium: SAR 4M/year
```
