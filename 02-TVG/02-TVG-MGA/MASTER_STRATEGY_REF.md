## 1. STRATEGIC MISSION

Nexus MGA is the **risk infrastructure layer** of the Theeb Ecosystem — Layer 2 of TVG's four-layer financial operating system. In the 2026 logic:

1. **Data-Driven Insurance Design:** Uses clinical intelligence from CII, claims data from Integra RCM, and outcomes data from VBH Co to design actuarially sound insurance products — not guesswork-based underwriting.
2. **Malpractice + CME Bundle Innovation:** The only insurance product in KSA that bundles malpractice coverage with SCFHS-accredited CME hours (SAR 800-1,200/physician/year) — a cross-group innovation combining TVG risk + TKG education.
3. **Payer-Provider Bridge:** Positions between providers (accessed via Integra RCM) and payers (accessed via SAMA licensing) — the intermediary that aligns incentives.

**Chess Player Role:** Nexus MGA occupies the risk design square. It translates data from Layer 1 (revenue) and Layer 3 (outcomes) into insurance products that align payer and provider incentives — the critical mechanism for resolving the 83-96% loss ratio crisis.
