## Design Notes (for NotebookLM or Designer)

**Overall tone:**
- Authoritative, institutional, prestigious — this targets society board members and presidents
- Should feel like a strategic partnership proposal, not a sales pitch
- The society should feel they are being invited to an exclusive infrastructure program

**Colors:**
- Deep navy (#0A1F3F) + Gold (#B8860B) + White
- Premium academic feel — think university or royal institution

**Fonts:**
- Headlines: Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond (serif, prestigious)
- Body: Inter or Source Sans Pro (clean, readable)

**Images:**
- Medical conferences with Saudi physicians
- Journal covers (generic/representative)
- Academic award ceremonies
- Scientific society meetings

**Layout:**
- 8 pages (can be reduced to 6 by combining pages 3-4)
- Landscape or square format for digital sharing
- Each page standalone-shareable

**Key visual:**
- A scoring thermometer or progress bar showing the path from Category C → Category A
- Before/After comparison visual (society without Theeb vs with Theeb)

**QR Code:**
- Last page → partnership inquiry form (society name, specialty, current category, president name, phone)

**Critical notes:**
- Emphasize "zero cost to society" and "you keep 40-60% of revenue" prominently
- Never position Theeb as taking over the society — always "behind the scenes"
- The society keeps ALL credit, authorship, and reputation
- Use the phrase "infrastructure partner" not "service provider"
