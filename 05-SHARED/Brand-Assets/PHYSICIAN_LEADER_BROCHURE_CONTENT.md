## Design Notes (for NotebookLM or Designer)

**Overall tone:**
- Premium, professional, academic — not commercial or salesy
- The physician should feel this was designed personally for them, not a generic brochure
- No prices mentioned — this is a relationship brochure, not a sales flyer

**Colors:**
- Dark blue (#1E3A5F) + Gold (#C9A84C) + White
- Alternative: Deep navy + Silver + White

**Fonts:**
- Headlines: Montserrat Bold or Playfair Display
- Body: Inter, IBM Plex Sans, or Source Sans Pro
- If Arabic version needed later: Tajawal Bold (headlines) + IBM Plex Arabic (body)

**Images:**
- Physicians in premium professional environments
- Medical conferences, lectures, modern clinics
- No generic stock photos — images should feel authentic and local

**Layout:**
- 6 pages (square 1:1 slides for social media sharing, or A4 for direct sending)
- Each page works as a standalone unit (any single page can be shared independently)
- Simple icons for each service

**QR Code:**
- On the last page, directing to a simple interest registration form (name, specialty, phone)

**Critical note:**
- Do NOT mention specific journal names — say "local and international indexed journals"
- Do NOT limit the physician — emphasize that we select with them the best journal for their promotion
- Focus on building the entity, not selling services
- The word "service" should appear minimally — use "build," "create," "design," "partner" instead
