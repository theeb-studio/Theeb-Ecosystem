## 1. STRATEGIC MISSION

Nama CRO is the **primary data generator** of the Theeb Ecosystem — the clinical research execution engine that produces structured, high-quality Saudi clinical data. In the 2026 logic:

1. **Evidence Generation:** Executes Phase I-IV trials, observational studies, and real-world evidence studies that feed every downstream entity with clinical data.
2. **Flywheel Starter:** Every trial Nama CRO runs generates data that feeds the Evidence Platform's knowledge graph, Waraqa's publication pipeline, and CII's AI training corpus — one input, multiple value outputs.
3. **Strategic Investor Integration:** Alfaisaliah Group investment ($5-7M) brings guaranteed CRO revenue from their pharmaceutical portfolio — de-risking the revenue model from Day 1.

**Chess Player Role:** Nama CRO is the piece that generates the raw material (clinical data) the entire board runs on. Without locally generated evidence, the ecosystem would depend on imported knowledge — defeating the sovereignty thesis.
