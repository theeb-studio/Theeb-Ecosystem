# TKG-EVP — EVIDENCE PLATFORM
**Entity Code: TKG-05 | Group: Theeb Knowledge Group**

---

## Identity
The Evidence Platform is Saudi Arabia's **sovereign clinical evidence platform** — the digital infrastructure layer for clinical guidelines, accredited CME, real-world data, and a Saudi-native clinical knowledge graph. It connects evidence producers (researchers, societies) with evidence consumers (physicians, hospitals, payers).

## Role in TKG
- Distributes all TKG content digitally at national scale
- Hosts SCFHS-accredited CME courses (Nama Academy content delivered digitally)
- Provides the knowledge graph and evidence infrastructure used by Nama CRO
- Creates the digital product that scientific society members access daily

## Core Platform Capabilities
| Module | Function |
|--------|----------|
| Knowledge Graph | Saudi-native clinical knowledge (SNOMED CT Saudi extension, Neo4j) |
| CME Academy | SCFHS-accredited digital CME — 30 credits/year fulfillment |
| Guidelines Platform | Living clinical guidelines with automatic updates |
| RWD / Evidence | Real-world data analytics for pharma and payers |
| Society Hub | Digital infrastructure for each scientific society partner |
| Research Portal | Protocol submission, systematic review tools, publication pipeline |

## Target Users
- Physicians and HCPs (CME, clinical decision support)
- Scientific societies (member management, CME, journals)
- Pharma companies (RWD analytics, CME sponsorship)
- Regulators (SCFHS CME data, CHI outcomes data)

## Independence Roadmap
```

Phase 2 (Next):    Separate legal entity formed under TKG

```

## Integration Points
- Nama Academy → provides accredited content and course catalog
- Nama CRO → feeds evidence and systematic reviews
- Waraqa → publishes guidelines hosted on the platform
- TVG MGA → CME hours fulfilled through platform for insurance bundle
- THDG Welunion → physician users; clinical data feeds back to knowledge graph
