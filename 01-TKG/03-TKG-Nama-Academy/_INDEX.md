# TKG-AKD — NAMA ACADEMY
**Entity Code: TKG-03 | Group: Theeb Knowledge Group**

---

## Identity
Nama Academy is the **healthcare training and education company** of TKG — delivering accredited CME/CPD programs, life support certification (BLS/ACLS/ATLS), simulation-based training, and institutional education infrastructure for hospitals, scientific societies, and insurance-bundled products across Saudi Arabia.

## Role in TKG
- Delivers CME on the Evidence Platform's content (digital + physical)
- Provides the educational delivery arm for Waraqa's scientific society partnerships
- Partners with TVG MGA to fulfill the CME component of malpractice insurance bundles
- Supplies Nama CRO with research-trained clinical staff pipeline

## Key Strategic Assets
- **SCFHS CPD/CME accreditation** — held and active
- **GM leads the Saudi Heart Association** — direct access to SHA's NCPR committee (BLS/CPR since 1984) and 2,000+ cardiologist members
- **Asset-light simulation model** — rents underutilized simulation centers across KSA; no capital investment in own lab

## Business Model
| Revenue Stream | Mechanism |
|----------------|-----------|
| Hospital white-label academies | SAR 200–500K/year licensing — society or hospital uses Nama's accreditation |

## Scientific Societies Model
Nama + Waraqa approach Saudi scientific societies (80+ targets) as **partners, not clients**:
- Zero upfront fee — revenue share on all streams generated
- Nama provides: CME accreditation, course delivery, simulation, BLS/ACLS
- Waraqa provides: journal upgrade, guidelines platform, ERP/management
- Society provides: brand, members, content, conference infrastructure
- Target outcome: Society moves from SCFHS Category C/D (320 pts) → Category A (1,150+ pts)

## Anchor Partnership
- **Saudi Heart Association** — GM leads SHA; SHA is the proof-of-concept for all other societies
- NCPR committee (SHA) → national BLS/CPR infrastructure since 1984

## TVG MGA Integration
TVG MGA designs a malpractice insurance product bundled with 30–60 Nama Academy CME hours:
- MGA earns underwriting commission
- Nama earns per-physician CME fulfillment fee (SAR 800–1,200/physician/year)
- Physician gets legal protection + SCFHS compliance in one purchase
