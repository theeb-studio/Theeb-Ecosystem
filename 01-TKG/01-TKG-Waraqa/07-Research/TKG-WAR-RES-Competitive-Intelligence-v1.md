### Total Government Spend Estimate

| Period | Estimated SDL Spend | Notes |
|--------|-------------------|-------|
| 2020-2023 (3-year cycle) | SAR 80-100M | Multiple partial awards |

### Key Observations

1. **All winners are resellers** — none produce content
2. **iDev is the dominant player** — wins the largest portions consistently
3. **Naseej competes on scale** — leverages listed-company resources
4. **Many bids come in at SAR 0.00** — indicating bundled or loss-leader pricing strategies
5. **Some bidders fail technical evaluation** — e.g., شركة أبحاث القابضة, شركة آجال السعودية (غير مطابق فنياً)
6. **SDL pricing appears inflated** — many hospitals and entities buy directly from publishers at lower prices

## 3. DIRECT PURCHASE MARKET (Non-SDL)

Many Saudi institutions bypass the SDL and purchase database subscriptions directly from publishers or through local agents. This is significant because:

1. **It indicates SDL pricing is perceived as inflated** — institutions find better deals directly
2. **The market is fragmented** — no single aggregator serves everyone
3. **Each hospital/university negotiates independently** — massive inefficiency
4. **No AI rights are included** — all purchases are reading-access only

### Estimated Direct Purchase Volume

| Buyer Type | Estimated Count | Avg Annual Spend | Total |
|-----------|----------------|-----------------|-------|
| Large private hospitals (KFSH, NMC, etc.) | 30 | SAR 500K-2M | 15M-60M |
| Medical cities | 5 | SAR 2M-5M | 10M-25M |
| Private universities | 15 | SAR 200K-1M | 3M-15M |
| Pharma companies (Saudi ops) | 20 | SAR 100K-500K | 2M-10M |
| **Estimated non-SDL total** | | | **SAR 30M-110M** |

**Combined market (SDL + direct):** SAR 60M-150M annually on reading access alone.

## 5. IMPLICATIONS FOR WARAQA

1. **Total content market (reading + AI) = SAR 100M-300M annually** — Waraqa can capture portions through content production + AI licensing
2. **iDev is the logical distribution partner** — they already serve the institutional buyers

4. **Direct institutional sales** — hospitals that buy directly are receptive to bundled offerings (content + AI)
5. **Government tender track** — Waraqa should register as a vendor on Etimad and bid on future SDL tenders (publishing services category)
