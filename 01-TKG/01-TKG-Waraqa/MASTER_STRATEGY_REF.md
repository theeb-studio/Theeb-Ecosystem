## 1. STRATEGIC MISSION

Waraqa is the **evidence governance engine** of the Theeb Ecosystem — the entity that transforms raw clinical data into validated, peer-reviewed, published knowledge. In the 2026 Saudi healthcare logic, Waraqa serves three critical functions:

1. **Scientific Sovereignty:** Replaces Saudi Arabia's dependence on imported Western clinical knowledge by producing locally generated, peer-reviewed evidence through society journals and clinical guidelines.
2. **Society Network Lock-In:** Through revenue-sharing partnerships with 80+ Saudi scientific societies, Waraqa systematically locks in the nation's top clinical experts and their physician networks as ecosystem assets.
3. **Content Supply Chain:** Feeds the Evidence Platform's knowledge graph and CII's clinical intelligence layer with the structured, validated content they require to function — making Waraqa the upstream content engine for the entire Shared Intelligence Layer.

**Chess Player Role:** Waraqa is the piece that controls the credibility square. Without peer-reviewed evidence, the ecosystem cannot claim scientific authority. Every guideline Waraqa publishes becomes a decision-support asset for CII, a curriculum for Nama Academy, and a benchmark for VBH Co.
