# WARAQA — PARTNERSHIP PLAYBOOK
**Entity Code: TKG-01 | Version 1.0 | March 2026**
**Classification: Confidential — Business Development**

---

> **Purpose:** A reusable framework for Waraqa partnership proposals.
> Replaces the practice of writing bespoke proposals from scratch for each partner.
> The CUA and KAU proposals (Dec 2025) served as the prototype for this playbook.

---

## 1. PARTNERSHIP TYPES

Waraqa pursues four categories of partnerships. Each has a different value exchange, activation model, and TKG entity involvement.

### Type A: University / Academic Institution

**What they get:** National preprint platform anchor status, research excellence infrastructure, journal indexing support
**What Waraqa gets:** Institutional credibility, editorial talent pool, research manuscript flow
**Financial model:** Zero cost to the institution — Waraqa invests and operates
**TKG entities activated:** Waraqa (lead) + Evidence Platform (technology)

**Targets:** CUA, KAU, KSU, KAUST, KFU, Alfaisal, Imam Mohammed bin Saud

### Type B: Scientific Society

**What they get:** Full publishing infrastructure — journal, conference proceedings, preprints, digital distribution, CME delivery
**What Waraqa gets:** Brand credibility, member access, editorial boards, clinical content
**Financial model:** Revenue share per stream (see Section 4)
**TKG entities activated:** Waraqa (lead) + Nama Academy (CME) + Evidence Platform (digital)

**Targets:** Saudi Heart Association (anchor), Saudi Thoracic Society, Saudi Diabetes Society, Saudi Ophthalmology Society, Saudi Gastro Society + 50 more registered societies

### Type C: Government Ministry / Regulator

**What they get:** National research infrastructure, journal indexing excellence, regulatory publishing
**What Waraqa gets:** National mandate, institutional backing, regulatory positioning
**Financial model:** Contract-based (funded by ministry) or investment model
**TKG entities activated:** Waraqa (lead) + Nama CRO (research) + Evidence Platform (technology)

**Targets:** MOE (journal excellence program), MOH (health systems research journal), SCFHS (CPD content), CCHI (quality publications)

### Type D: Research Funder / Foundation

**What they get:** Publishing arm — journal launch, editorial management, research dissemination
**What Waraqa gets:** Funded publishing mandate, prestige, long-term content pipeline
**Financial model:** Funded by partner — Waraqa operates on service + revenue share
**TKG entities activated:** Waraqa (lead) + Nama CRO (research support)

**Targets:** Hevolution Foundation (MOU active), KACST, SDAIA, Neom research programs

## 2. THE THREE-TRACK ENGAGEMENT MODEL

Every Waraqa partnership, regardless of type, follows this three-track structure:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT                         │
├──────────────────┬──────────────────────┬───────────────────────┤
│   TRACK 1        │   TRACK 2            │   TRACK 3             │
│   Technology     │   Platform           │   Editorial &         │
│   Stack          │   Operations         │   Scientific          │
│                  │                      │   Governance          │
├──────────────────┼──────────────────────┼───────────────────────┤
│ • Platform       │ • Editorial workflow │ • Advisory board      │
│   deployment     │ • Submission mgmt    │ • Peer review policy  │
│ • Hosting        │ • Production         │ • Research integrity  │
│ • Integration    │ • Indexing strategy  │ • Ethical standards   │
│ • Analytics      │ • Marketing/outreach │ • Quality assurance   │
├──────────────────┼──────────────────────┼───────────────────────┤
│ Waraqa +         │ Waraqa               │ Partner +             │
│ Evidence Platform│ (operations lead)    │ Waraqa (advisory)     │
└──────────────────┴──────────────────────┴───────────────────────┘
```

### Track Responsibilities by Partner Type

| Track | University | Society | Ministry | Funder |
|-------|-----------|---------|----------|--------|
| Track 1: Technology | Waraqa + TKG-05 | Waraqa + TKG-05 | Waraqa + TKG-05 | Waraqa |
| Track 2: Operations | Waraqa (full) | Waraqa (full) | Waraqa + ministry team | Waraqa (full) |
| Track 3: Governance | Joint (academic lead) | Joint (society lead) | Ministry-led | Joint |

## 3. VALUE PROPOSITION — BY PARTNER TYPE

### For Universities

> "Your university becomes the academic anchor of Saudi Arabia's first national preprint platform — at zero financial cost. You provide academic credibility and editorial expertise. We provide technology, operations, and international publishing standards."

**Key metrics to cite:**
- 3,748 Saudi papers impacted by Hindawi/MDPI delistings
- No national preprint server exists (China, Japan, India all have one)
- Vision 2030 R&D spend target: 2.5% of GDP
- Saudi Arabia's international research ranking at risk without infrastructure

### For Scientific Societies

> "Your society currently earns SAR 10,000/year from government incentives. With Waraqa's infrastructure, your society earns SAR 1.5M+ through journal publishing, CME delivery, conference management, and pharma partnerships — all on a revenue-share model with zero upfront cost."

**Key metrics to cite:**
- 50+ registered Saudi scientific societies
- SCFHS requires CME for all 300,000+ licensed HCPs
- Society scoring: jump from 320 points to 1,150+ (Category A status)
- Revenue increase: 150x from government incentive baseline

### For Government Ministries

> "We build the national infrastructure that makes Saudi research globally credible again. Journal indexing improvement, preprint infrastructure, research integrity systems — all aligned to Vision 2030."

### For Research Funders

> "Your funded research deserves a publishing pathway that keeps IP and data sovereign to Saudi Arabia. Waraqa provides editorial excellence, international standards, and full publishing operations — so your research investment doesn't end up in a foreign mega-publisher's database."

## 4. REVENUE SHARE FRAMEWORK

### Society Partnerships (Type B)

| Revenue Stream | Society Share | Waraqa/TKG Share | Notes |
|----------------|-------------|------------------|-------|
| Journal APC / subscription | 60% | 40% | Their journal, their brand |
| Pharma CME symposia | 40% | 60% | TKG does commercial work |
| Insurance CME bundle | 30% | 70% | TKG owns MGA relationship |
| Conference management | 50% | 50% | Co-produced |
| Digital CME delivery | 50% | 50% | Audience + content split |
| RWD / pharma analytics | 35% | 65% | FASL/Evidence Platform built it |
| Research grants (CRO) | 60% | 40% | Society PI leads research |

### University Partnerships (Type A)

| Component | University Cost | Revenue Model |
|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| Platform deployment | Zero | Waraqa invests |
| Ongoing operations | Zero | APC revenue share: University 60% / Waraqa 40% |
| Premium services | Negotiable | Per-project pricing |

### Funder Partnerships (Type D)

| Component | Model |
|-----------|-------|
| Journal operations | Funded by partner (cost + margin) |
| Revenue from APC/subscriptions | Shared: Funder 50% / Waraqa 50% |
| IP ownership | Waraqa retains operational IP; content IP per agreement |

## 5. IMPLEMENTATION TIMELINE — STANDARD

Applicable to all partnership types. Adjust per partner.

| Phase | Duration | Deliverables |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Discovery** | Week 1-2 | Needs assessment, stakeholder mapping, scope agreement |
| **Agreement** | Week 3-4 | Partnership terms, revenue model, governance structure |
| **Platform Setup** | Week 5-8 | Technology deployment, branding, editorial workflow configuration |
| **Editorial Board** | Week 5-8 | Board selection, scope definition, peer review policy |
| **Soft Launch** | Week 9-10 | Internal testing, first submissions, workflow validation |
| **Public Launch** | Week 11-12 | Marketing campaign, first publications, indexing applications |
| **Optimization** | Month 4-12 | Analytics review, indexing progression, volume growth |

## 7. GOVERNANCE TEMPLATE

Every partnership establishes:

| Body | Composition | Meeting Cadence |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| **Steering Committee** | 2 from partner + 2 from Waraqa | Quarterly |
| **Editorial Advisory Board** | 5-7 subject experts (partner-nominated) | Bi-annually |
| **Research Integrity Committee** | 3 members (1 international) | As needed |
| **Operations Team** | Waraqa-led, partner liaison | Weekly |

## 8. PROPOSAL GENERATION CHECKLIST

When preparing a new partnership proposal, use this checklist:

- [ ] Identify partnership type (A/B/C/D)
- [ ] Determine which TKG entities to activate
- [ ] Customize value proposition for partner's specific context
- [ ] Select relevant metrics from this playbook
- [ ] Draft using three-track model
- [ ] Include Vision 2030 alignment section
- [ ] Include research integrity crisis context (Nature citation)
- [ ] Define governance structure
- [ ] Set revenue share terms (from Section 4)
- [ ] Define success metrics (from Section 6)
- [ ] Review with Inspiring STEM (international credibility check)
