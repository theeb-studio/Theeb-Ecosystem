# TKG-WAR — WARAQA PUBLISHING HOUSE
**Entity Code: TKG-01 | Group: Theeb Knowledge Group**

---

## Identity
Waraqa is the **scientific publishing and knowledge infrastructure company** of TKG — providing peer-reviewed journals, clinical guidelines platforms, and publishing services for scientific societies and healthcare institutions across Saudi Arabia.

## Role in TKG
- Publishes clinical guidelines that Nama Academy teaches
- Hosts peer-reviewed journals for scientific societies
- Provides the publishing backbone for Evidence Platform content
- Leads the Scientific Societies Enablement Initiative (with Nama Academy)

## Important Note on Naming
Older documents may reference "Waraqa Platform" as a digital product.
That product is now properly classified as **TKG-05 Evidence Platform** — a separate entity.
- Waraqa (TKG-01) = the publishing house (company)
- Evidence Platform (TKG-05) = the digital platform (becoming independent)
