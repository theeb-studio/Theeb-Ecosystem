# PHYSICIAN REPUTATION & KNOWLEDGE PLATFORM
## Subscription-Based Career Management Infrastructure for Physicians
**Entity: TKG-01 (Waraqa) | Integrated with: Nama Academy (TKG-03), Evidence Platform (TKG-05), PubHelper (TKG-06)**
**Aligned to: THEEB_ECOSYSTEM_V2_2026.md | Chess Player Framework**
**Version 1.0 | March 2026 | Confidential**

---

> **CORE INSIGHT:** Physicians are the most valuable node in the Theeb Ecosystem — they prescribe, refer, research, teach, and influence. Yet most physicians lack the infrastructure to manage their professional reputation, produce credible public content, or grow academically. This platform gives them that infrastructure — and in doing so, locks each physician deeper into the ecosystem across Waraqa (publishing), Nama Academy (CME), Evidence Platform (clinical intelligence), and PubHelper (research).

---

## 1. THE PROBLEM

| Challenge | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| **Limited time** | Physicians cannot manage professional presence alongside clinical duties |
| **No content infrastructure** | Producing scientifically credible educational content requires writers, editors, designers, videographers — physicians don't have teams |
| **Academic bottleneck** | Most physicians want to publish but lack research protocol support, statistical analysis, and manuscript preparation |
| **Misinformation gap** | Social media is flooded with unverified health content; credible physician voices are drowned out |
| **Invisible expertise** | The best physicians in KSA are often invisible to the public, to industry, and to their own profession |

**This model ensures:** No misinformation. Every public post is traceable to evidence. The physician's credibility is protected and enhanced.

The physician subscribes to the Reputation Platform → their research needs are routed to PubHelper → output is published via Waraqa journals → feeds Live Guidelines Platform → generates CME credits via Evidence Platform. One subscription triggers the entire knowledge pipeline.

## 6. STRATEGIC EVOLUTION

### Phase 1: Physician Subscription Platform (2026-2027)
Individual physician subscriptions across three tiers. Focus on 150 early adopters from scientific society partnerships.

### Phase 2: Institutional Packages (2027-2028)
Hospital groups subscribe on behalf of their physician staff. Integrate with AaaS (Nama Academy) contracts — hospital buys training + physician reputation as one package.

### Phase 3: Medical Knowledge & Influence Network (2028+)
Platform evolves into a **physician knowledge marketplace** connecting:
- Physicians with research opportunities (PubHelper)
- Physicians with teaching opportunities (Nama Academy)
- Physicians with industry partnerships (advisory boards, expert panels)
- Physicians with public health campaigns (government, NGO)
- Physicians with each other (peer networks, communities of practice)

This network becomes a **strategic asset** for:
- Real-world evidence generation (physician-reported practice data)
- Medical education initiatives (physician-created CME content)
- Public health campaigns (physician-led health communication)
- Research collaboration (multi-site physician networks)
