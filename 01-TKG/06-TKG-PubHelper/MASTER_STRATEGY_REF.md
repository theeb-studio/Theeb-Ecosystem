PubHelper is the engine that converts **one question into five assets** — research, publication, guideline, MNC criteria, and payment logic.

---

## 1. STRATEGIC MISSION — ONE QUESTION TO ONE EVIDENCE

PubHelper is the **AI-powered research factory** that sits at the interface between Service Delivery (clinicians' daily questions) and Knowledge Building (structured evidence). It converts raw clinical curiosity into publishable, graded evidence that immediately feeds the Live Guidelines Platform.

1. **Research-as-a-Service:** Captures clinician questions (text, voice, templates), converts them into PICO research projects, manages the full lifecycle from protocol to publication.
2. **Live Guidelines Feeder:** Every completed project auto-feeds into the Live Guidelines Platform — updating the Saudi-specific evidence base for that condition.
3. **Community Knowledge Capture:** Formalizes expert discussions (300+ member WhatsApp groups, society forums) into structured research questions.
4. **Evidence Gap Detector:** Works with the Evidence Platform's knowledge graph to identify where Saudi-specific evidence is missing and prioritize research.

**Chess Player Role:** PubHelper converts the ecosystem's most abundant raw material — clinical questions from 300,000+ healthcare professionals — into its most valuable asset: locally generated, graded evidence. Without PubHelper, questions remain questions. With PubHelper, every question becomes intellectual property.

## 4. EVIDENCE GRADING RUBRIC (Quality Gate)

| Dimension | Level 1: Emerging | Level 3: Strong | Level 5: Apex |
|-----------|------------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Study Design** | Case reports, expert opinions | Prospective cohort/case-control | Meta-analysis/SR of RCTs |
| **Methodology** | Descriptive; no control | PICO; risk-adjusted; p<0.05 | Double-blinded; multi-center; low bias |
| **Data Integrity** | Manual; small sample | eCRF; >85% complete; ICD verified | Federated multi-site; independent audit |
| **Local Relevance** | General; no Saudi data | Saudi cohorts (Welunion) | High-priority disease with policy impact |
| **Publication** | Non-indexed | Scopus/PubMed indexed | National Guideline (Waraqa/CHI) |

**SOR Scoring:** Grade A (20-25) = national guidelines | Grade B (15-19) = institutional protocols | Grade C (<15) = emerging evidence
