### 2.2 Revenue Stream Detail

**Stream 1: SCFHS National Contract**
- SCFHS currently pays SAR 20M+/year for UpToDate with 15-30% utilization
- Saudi Evidence offers: free platform + Arabic + Saudi-first + CME integration + workforce analytics
- Pitch: redirect 30-50% of UpToDate budget → Saudi Evidence government contract
- Y2: Pilot contract SAR 2M; Y3: National rollout SAR 6M; Y5: Full contract SAR 10M
- **This is a REPLACEMENT budget** — SCFHS doesn't need new money, just reallocation

**Stream 2: Hospital B2B SaaS**
- Hospitals subscribe for: institutional dashboard, workforce analytics, CBAHI readiness, department-level competency mapping
- Tiered pricing: Small hospital SAR 50K/yr | Medium SAR 100K/yr | Large SAR 150K/yr | Teaching SAR 200K/yr
- 565 hospitals in Saudi Arabia; target 500 by Y5 (88% penetration)
- When platform is free to ALL clinicians, hospitals get massive value from institutional analytics layer
- **Key insight:** If your clinicians are already using it daily for free, the institutional subscription is a no-brainer

**Stream 3: Pharma Data & Insights**
- De-identified, aggregated data packages:
  - What clinical questions are physicians asking? (Trending evidence gaps)
  - Which drugs are being searched most? (Prescribing intent signals)
  - Where are evidence gaps in Saudi clinical practice? (Market opportunity identification)
  - KOL mapping: which physicians are most active in which therapeutic areas?
- No individual data sold — only population-level, aggregated, PDPL-compliant analytics
- SAR 300-500K per pharma company per therapeutic area per year
- 20+ pharma companies × 2-3 therapeutic areas each = SAR 15M by Y5

**Stream 4: CME Marketplace**
- Saudi Evidence hosts CME courses from: Nama Academy, scientific societies, pharma-sponsored education, hospital CME programs
- Platform takes 30% commission; content provider keeps 70%
- 460K registered users × 10% purchasing CME annually × SAR 500 avg course = SAR 23M gross marketplace revenue
- Platform commission at 30% = SAR 6.9M → modeled conservatively at SAR 8M by Y5

**Stream 5: API & EHR Integration**
- HIS/EHR vendors license Saudi Evidence API for in-workflow clinical decision support
- SAR 50-100K per vendor per year + per-query usage fees
- Major HIS vendors in Saudi: Cerner, Epic, InterSystems, CompuGroup, local systems
- WhatsApp integration: healthcare-specific bot with evidence lookup (licensing to telehealth providers)

**Stream 6: CHI MNC Engine License**
- The MNC Decision Engine (70 CHI criteria for medical necessity) is unique infrastructure
- CHI needs this deployed across ALL payers and providers — licensing model
- SAR 2M Y3 pilot → SAR 6M Y5 at national scale
