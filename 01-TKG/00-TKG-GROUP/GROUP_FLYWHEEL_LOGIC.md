## HOW THE 5 ENTITIES REINFORCE EACH OTHER

### Loop 1: Evidence вҶ’ Publication вҶ’ Distribution

```
Nama CRO runs trial вҶ’ produces structured clinical data
    вҶ’ Waraqa publishes results in peer-reviewed journal
    вҶ’ Evidence Platform indexes into knowledge graph
    вҶ’ Physicians access at point of care
    вҶ’ Evidence gaps detected вҶ’ new research priorities
    вҶ’ Nama CRO designs next trial вҶ’ [LOOP REPEATS]
```

**Compound effect:** Each loop adds knowledge nodes, publications, and physician engagement вҖ” the graph grows exponentially.

### Loop 2: Training вҶ’ Distribution вҶ’ Research

```
Nama Academy trains 2,000+ physicians/year
    вҶ’ Each receives Evidence Platform account ($0 CAC)
    вҶ’ Physicians use platform for clinical decisions
    вҶ’ Platform captures questions and reveals evidence gaps
    вҶ’ Evidence gaps become Nama CRO research priorities
    вҶ’ Trial results feed Waraqa publications
    вҶ’ New publications become Nama Academy curriculum
    вҶ’ [LOOP REPEATS with larger physician base]
```

**Compound effect:** Physician network grows linearly while platform value grows exponentially.

### Loop 3: Drug Localization вҶ’ Publication вҶ’ Intelligence

```
Nama Bio runs BE study вҶ’ produces Saudi PK/PD data
    вҶ’ Waraqa publishes results
    вҶ’ Evidence Platform adds drug data to knowledge graph
    вҶ’ CII uses data for prescribing intelligence
    вҶ’ Physicians make better prescribing decisions
    вҶ’ More drugs localized вҶ’ more data вҶ’ [LOOP REPEATS]
```

**Compound effect:** Saudi population pharmacokinetic data is irreplaceable вҖ” each study makes the dataset more valuable.

### Loop 4: Society Partnerships вҶ’ Multi-Entity Revenue

```
Waraqa partners with scientific society
    вҶ’ Launches society journal (40-60% revenue share)
    вҶ’ Nama Academy co-organizes society CME (40-50% revenue share)
    вҶ’ Evidence Platform hosts society digital hub
    вҶ’ Nama CRO recruits society experts as PIs
    вҶ’ Society reaches Category A SCFHS rating
    вҶ’ More societies see the model вҶ’ more partnerships
    вҶ’ [LOOP REPEATS across 80+ societies]
```

**Compound effect:** Each society partnership generates revenue for 4 TKG entities simultaneously.
