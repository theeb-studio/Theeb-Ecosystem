| Old Mental Model (Separate) | New Mental Model (Unified) |

| Nama Academy = training company | Nama Academy = the **human capability layer** |
| Evidence Platform = clinical tool | Evidence Platform = the **digital capability backbone** |
| Handoff: account creation after training | No handoff: **same system, continuous lifecycle** |
| CME is a Nama product delivered on Evidence Platform | CME, clinical decisions, research, and competency tracking are **one integrated experience** |

## PART FIVE: THE WORKFORCE DATA ASSET

### What the Unified System Knows

When every healthcare professional is inside the Nama + Evidence Platform system, the ecosystem accumulates the **most valuable workforce dataset in Saudi healthcare**:

| Data Layer | What It Contains | Strategic Value |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Individual Competency** | Per-professional skill levels, gaps, learning history | Workforce planning for hospitals, SCFHS, MOH |
| **Institutional Capability** | Aggregated competency per hospital/department | CBAHI readiness, quality benchmarking |
| **National Workforce Intelligence** | Specialty distribution, training gaps, certification rates | Policy input for SCFHS, MOH, Vision 2030 |
| **Learning Behavior** | What evidence professionals access, what questions they ask | Curriculum optimization, evidence gap detection |
| **Clinical-Training Correlation** | How training impacts clinical decision quality | Proof that training → better outcomes (for VBH Co) |

### Why This Data Is Unreplicable

No standalone training company or standalone evidence platform can generate this dataset. It requires:
1. Live training delivery (Nama Academy) — to capture competency
2. Daily clinical usage (Evidence Platform) — to capture practice patterns
3. Research contribution (PubHelper) — to capture knowledge generation
4. Outcomes measurement (VBH Co) — to correlate training with results
5. Revenue data (Integra RCM) — to correlate competency with financial performance

Only the Theeb Ecosystem has all five data sources in one integrated system.
